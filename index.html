The path

Four steps from idea to operating team.

01

Map the leak.

Find the workflow where follow-up, context, or manual coordination creates measurable drag.

02

Design the team.

Name the agents, define their lanes, memory, tools, cadence, and approval boundaries.

03

Launch narrow.

Ship the first agent into one workflow with visible outputs and human review.

04

Compound.

Turn repeated fixes into memory, SOPs, monitoring, and new agents only when the handoff is obvious.
